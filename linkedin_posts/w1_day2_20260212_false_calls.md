💸 The hidden cost nobody talks about: False Calls.

Industry data shows traditional AOI systems generate up to 90% false call rates.

That means for every 10 flags your AOI raises, 9 are wrong.

The real cost isn't just the operator reviewing them.
It's:
→ Production slowdowns
→ Operator fatigue → missed REAL defects
→ Loss of trust in the inspection system
→ Engineers constantly re-tuning algorithms

When your operators stop trusting the AOI, they stop looking carefully.
That's when real defects slip through.

False calls aren't just annoying. They're dangerous.

What if AI could learn to tell the difference? 🤔
